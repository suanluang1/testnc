A Royal Legacy

Suanluang Rama IX, also referred to as King Rama IX Park, is Bangkok's largest public park spanning 500 rai (80 hectares) in the Prawet District.

Established in 1987 to commemorate King Bhumibol Adulyadej's 60th birthday, this botanical paradise was officially opened by the King himself on December 1, 1987.
